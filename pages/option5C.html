Ruined Temple

Arriving at the temple (via spirits' secret path, or main path):

Successfully retrieve the amulet:
With the amulet in hand, Elara returns to her village, greeted as a hero. She uses its powers to heal the land and communicate with the spirits, bringing prosperity.

Trigger a trap:
A floor tile sinks under her foot, and walls start closing in. Elara must find an escape route or use her gathered tools and knowledge to stop the trap.

Leave the temple without the amulet:
Realizing the risks are too great without more preparation, Elara decides to return home. She plans to gather more information and perhaps seek allies for another attempt.
