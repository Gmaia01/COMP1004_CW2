Whispering Woods

Entering the woods (from history book or librarian's poem):

Follow the path indicated by the poem:
The woods are alive with eerie sounds. Following the path laid out by the poem, Elara encounters woodland spirits who ask her to prove her worth by answering riddles about nature.

Explore freely:
Opting to trust her instincts, Elara wanders off the beaten path. She stumbles upon an overgrown trail lined with strange, glowing flowers that seem to beckon her deeper into the forest.
